    Meeting with Dr. Florian Kraft

    Next-generation sequencing is a modern sequencing method, which enables massively parallel sequencing of numerous genes and has thus revolutionized genetic diagnostics in recent years. Dr. Florian Kraft, post-doc at the Institut for human genetic in the uni hospital in Aachen, deals precisely with New Sequencing techniques, especially Nanopore sequencing, which is our preferrable method to analyze our synthesized sequences. Before our meeting and towards the end of our project we exchanged a lot of E-Mail’s and asked Dr. Kraft many questions about our samples for Nanopore sequencing, which he was always happy to answer. During our meeting in the middle of April, he explained the requirements for Nanopore-sequencing, such as the right primer length for Nanopore-sequencing. He raised the issue that using Homopolymers may be difficult because the nanopore could not detect the length of the polymer. Furthermore, we assessed the problem of adding his double-stranded nanopore adapters to our single-stranded DNA. Eventually, he proposed the opportunity to sequence our samples at the uni hospital at a low cost. We gladly accepted his offer and want to thank Dr. Kraft again for the great opportunity!
