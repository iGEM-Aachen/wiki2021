Are We About to Face a Digital Dark Age?

Loss of data poses a serious risk to infrastructure and safety. Whenever new storage technologies emerge, there is a risk that old media will eventually become unreadable.

The Solution:

Storing Data on DNA

With the unique properties of DNA and rapidly advancing technologies, we think that DNA data storage will replace existing solutions.

Extremely High Storage Capacity

The information density of DNA is 20000 times higher than the capacity of current storage technologies. With that, you could fit the information of the whole internet into an ordinary bucket. This could make many data centers obsolete.

What Could we Achieve with Better Storage Systems?

Bring Artificial Intelligence to the Next Level

Training neural networks requires huge amounts of data. With a larger storage capacity, the development of better systems can be accelerated.

Efficient Data Transport for Colonization of Planets

If humanity one day becomes an interplanetary species, it will be necessary to bring lots of information to space. Since weight is always a huge concern in space, DNA would be an ideal choice as a storage medium for this.
